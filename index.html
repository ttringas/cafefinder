Why CafeFinder Exists

I recently spent over a year traveling the world while learning to code, doing freelance web development, and prototyping my solar energy startup SolarList. I had a lot of fun, but I was also quite serious about staying productive and getting work done. Every time I went to a new city I had a mad scramble to find a suitable cafe for doing work. A good cafe has the following:

  • Fast (preferably free) Wifi
  • Power Outlets
  • Plenty of comfortable seating
  • Food & Restrooms
  • And most importantly a freelancer-friendly vibe where you won't get glared at for hanging around for a few hours.
